🚫 Prohibited 🚫

If you engage in any of the actions listed below, you will be promptly asked to leave the restaurant and may be restricted from future entry.

  • Waiting for maids to come in and out
  • Conversations with maids outside of the store, stalking
  • Soliciting the maid's private information (real name, contact information, etc.)
  • Verbal abuse, sexual comments, or harassing behavior toward maids
  • Entering the establishment in a state of intoxicatione
  • Photographing, video recording, or recording in the store without permission
  • Touching the maids

⭐ Dress appropriately ⭐

Please dress appropriately when visiting the maid cafe.

⌚ Seating ⌚

We have both indoor and outdoor seating available.

Please be considerate of other customers and limit your seating time to 30 minutes during peak hours.

🍴 Ordering process 🍴

Our maid cafe features a specific menu for food and drinks. Follow the ordering process and pay attention to any special instructions given by the maids.

✅ Respect other customers ✅

Be mindful of other customers in the cafe and avoid behavior that may disturb their enjoyment of the cafe.

☺ ♥ Have fun!!!!!!!! ♥ ☺

Lastly, enjoy your time at the maid cafe! The maids are there to provide a unique and enjoyable experience, so make the most of it while respecting the rules and everyone else in the cafe.
